Postdoctoral researcher, ACCUMES, INRIA, Sophia Antipolis
February, 2014 to February 2015

My research involved developing ﬁnite volume numerical schemes for non–local systems of conservation laws in multi dimensions with applications speciﬁc to pedestrian ﬂows. The numerical simulations on one and two dimensions were performed on MATLAB and Python.
